About me

Who I Am

Hi, I’m Gabby. I’m a student studying Creative Technology and Design, where I explore the space between visual design, engineering, and interactive systems. I’ve always been creative and enjoy making beautiful things, but I’m also deeply drawn to logic and structure. I like knowing there’s a right solution, or at least a better one. This field allows me to combine both.

How I Work

I approach every project function-first. If something isn’t usable, it isn’t successful. I care about clarity, organization, and thoughtful interaction. I strive to balance structure with play, ensuring that the work I create is both usable and enjoyable.

I’m detail-oriented and love refining interactions until they feel intuitive. What frustrates me most is bad design that gets in the way of the user experience, and I enjoy finding creative solutions to real-world problems that make life easier.

What I Make

My work spans UX design, graphic design, product design, coding, and physical prototyping. I especially enjoy building things—digitally or physically—and seeing projects come together from concept to reality. I’m also excited to explore animation and interactive media as part of my future design work.

Portfolio Note

This portfolio was fully designed and coded by me using HTML and CSS. Building it from scratch reflects my hands-on approach to design and implementation, where I care about both structure and user experience.
